Methodology: each year's IRR solves start · (1+r) + deposits · (1+r)½ = ending, i.e. deposits are treated as arriving mid-year. Overall IRR is the money-weighted return across all years (initial balance at the start, each year's deposits at that year's midpoint, final balance at the end). Benchmark paths apply the identical cash flows at the benchmark's returns — real calendar-year returns where the series has data, the flat rate elsewhere.
